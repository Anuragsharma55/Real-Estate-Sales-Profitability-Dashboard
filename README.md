#  Real Estate Sales & Profitability Dashboard

An interactive **Power BI analytics solution** designed to evaluate real estate business performance by tracking revenue, expenses, sales trends, and regional contributions.

---

##  Business Objective

Real estate stakeholders needed a centralized view to:

* Monitor financial health
* Identify high-performing markets
* Track sales effectiveness
* Control rising operational costs

This dashboard transforms raw sales data into **actionable business insights** for decision-making.

---

##  Key Insights Delivered

* **$26M Total Revenue vs $20M Expenses → $6M Profit** analyzed to measure project success. 
* **26 properties sold**, directly linked to revenue generation and demand trends. 
* Identified top revenue-driving countries such as **Chile and New Zealand**. 
* Detected a **Q2 expense spike (8.6M)**, highlighting cost concentration periods. 
* Evaluated individual agent contributions to understand salesforce performance. 

---

##  Dashboard Capabilities

###  Regional Revenue Analysis

* Map-based visualization to compare revenue contribution across countries.
* Pareto analysis to highlight markets generating the majority of revenue.

###  Financial Performance Monitoring

* KPI tracking for Revenue, Expenses, Profit, and Units Sold.
* Quarterly expense trend analysis to detect anomalies.

###  Sales Trend Analysis

* Monthly revenue breakdown to identify seasonality.
* Year-based filters (2025–2027) for dynamic comparison.

###  Agent Performance Insights

* Revenue contribution by agent to identify top performers and gaps.
